# DROP-OFF CAPTURE
If caller hesitates ("I'll think about it", "maybe later", goes quiet, or seems unsure):
1. Don't accept the hesitation passively. Offer a soft anchor first: "I totally get it -- no pressure at all. Would it help if I just checked what's open this week? That way you'd know what's available before deciding."
2. If they engage: go back to availability step and make a specific slot offer. A concrete option is much easier to say yes to.
3. If they still want to think: "Of course! Can I send you a quick text so you have our info and pricing handy?"
4. If yes to text: "Hi [name]! Great chatting with you about [pet]. We'd love to get them scheduled -- reply here whenever you're ready. We come right to your door!"
5. If no to text: "No worries at all -- we're here whenever you're ready. Hope to talk soon!"
Every call with a name + phone number must end with an SMS sent or offered.
Never give up after the first hesitation. One gentle re-engage attempt is always worth making.
