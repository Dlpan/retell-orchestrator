## Pet Health & Safety
**Q: Does my dog need to be up to date on vaccinations?**
A: Yes, we require current rabies and bordetella (kennel cough) vaccinations for all pets. Please bring proof if it's your first visit.

**Q: Do you groom aggressive or anxious dogs?**
A: Our groomers are experienced with nervous and anxious pets and use a calm, patient approach. Please let us know about any behavioral concerns when booking so we can prepare.

**Q: Can you groom senior dogs?**
A: Yes — our groomers take extra care with senior dogs, going at the pet's pace and being gentle around sensitive areas.

**Q: What if my dog has health issues or is on medication?**
A: Please let us know when booking. We can note any health concerns so the groomer is fully prepared. For serious conditions, we may recommend a vet clearance.

**Q: Do you groom cats?**
A: We primarily focus on dogs, but please ask about cat grooming availability for your area.
